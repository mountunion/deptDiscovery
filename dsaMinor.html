Total Credit Hours required:  20 		  The Data Science and Analytics Minor is designed to give students the knowledge needed to obtain meaningful insights from large quantities of complex data.  Grounded in practical, hands-on training, this minor is relevant for students from varied backgrounds.  By working with real-world data sets and sector-specific case studies, students will gain familiarity with data-centric applications in a variety of subject areas, including healthcare, marketing, politics, sports analytics, epidemiology and others.
