Total Credit Hours required:  81 		  The demand for software developers increases every day, and especially so for people who know how to design software for different devices -- smart phones, tablets and computers.  From programming and database theory to web development, the Multi-Platform Software Development major will immerse you in the technological skills required for success in this growing field.  Course requirements include a multi-person team software development project, and we strongly recommend that students participate in off-campus internship experiences.  When combined with our strong liberal arts foundation, you will not only be technically prepared for your career, you also will be equipped with the effective communication and leadership skills valued in today's professional world.

The optional concentration in Artificial Intelligence offers additional coursework and specialization in the field of artificial intelligence to students majoring in Multi-Platform Software Development.  In addition to the requirements for the MPSD major, students pursuing the Concentration in Artificial Intelligence must complete an additional 31 credit hours of coursework.
