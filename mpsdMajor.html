 		  What is the MPSD major all about?

    Two additional CSC courses numbered 300 or above
    CSC 491   Software Engineering Fundamentals
    CSC 492   The Practice of Software Engineering

    CSW 150   Web Development Fundamentals
    CSW 223   Programming for Interactive Media
    CSW 423   Interaction Design

    MTH 123   Elementary Statistics
    MTH 125   Elementary Discrete Mathematics
   The demand for software developers increases every day, and especially so for people who know how to design software for different devices -- smart phones, tablets and computers.  From programming and database theory to web development, the Multi-Platform Software Development major will immerse you in the technological skills required for success in this growing field.  Course requirements include a multi-person team software development project, and we strongly recommend that students participate in off-campus internship experiences.  When combined with our strong liberal arts foundation, you will not only be technically prepared for your career, you also will be equipped with the effective communication and leadership skills valued in today's professional world.
